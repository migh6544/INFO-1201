Stereotypes¶

Stereotypes can be seen as the problem child of human survival instinct. Though sometimes help avoid unwanted outcomes, stereotypes can steer a person away from an objectives reality. Unfortunately, this is as much true in the real world as it is in the fictional world. Accordingly, this project aims to elaborate on the importance of developing one's computational thinking and objective analysis to avoid negative stereotypes.¶
